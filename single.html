Building Overview

Overview

Wasserman Capital Partners, as exclusive agent, is pleased to offer for sale the fee simple interest in Intellicenter (the “Property”), a three-story, 250,000 square foot, class A Silver LEED Certified building located in Atlanta's business district. The property features a modern design, which utilizes the latest building technologies and offers reduced operating costs and cleaner air. IntelliCenter offers state-of-the-art Class A office space.

IntelliCenter provides easy access to a rapidly growing, well-educated workforce entry level to senior executive housing, as well as an abundance of retail, personal services, restaurants and overnight or extended stay hotels.

Along with a LEED Silver certification, the IntelliCenter Atlanta boasts an ENERGY STAR performance rating of 92. It currently ranks in the top 10 percent of buildings nationally in regards to energy efficiency.
